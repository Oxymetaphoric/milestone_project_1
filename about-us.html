Who are we?

Meet the people!

I've been a dog lover all my life, as when I was a toddler, we had a West Highland white terrier, that I called “No-No” because I couldn't say Snowy! He was my first but since then I've always had dogs in my life; they give you so much love and loyalty! I started working at the kennels, where my pony was also stabled, when I was just 14 years old. Looking after the Labradors, Retrievers and Norfolk Terriers was just my perfect day and over the last 19 years I've become a dog breeder and started breeding puppies. All the dogs I breed from are selected for their soft temperament and loveable nature; so I specialise in Labrador Retrievers, Golden Retrievers, Goldendoodles, Cavalier King Charles Spaniels and Cavapoos.

Meet the dogs!

My girls are my babies, I take steps to make sure both parents and babies are in good health throughout pregnancy all the way to their new home by ensuring:

  • each mum only has 2/3 litters and is then spayed
  • the mums & pups live inside the home with 24/7 care
  • the mums continue to live in family home when no longer breeding as they are part of my family
  • only the best puppy parents will be chosen
